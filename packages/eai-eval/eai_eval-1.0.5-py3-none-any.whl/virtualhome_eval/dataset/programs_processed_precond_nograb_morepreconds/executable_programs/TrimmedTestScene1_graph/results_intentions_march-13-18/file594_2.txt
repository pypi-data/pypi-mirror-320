Wash dishes by hand
Make some dish water, grab a dish rag, put dishes in dish water and wash.


[RUN] <sink> (1.42)
[FIND] <dish_soap> (1.1000)
[GRAB] <dish_soap> (1.1000)
[FIND] <plate> (1.1001)
[PUTBACK] <dish_soap> (1.1000) <plate> (1.1001)
[RINSE] <dish_soap> (1.1000)
[FIND] <plate> (1.1001)
[WASH] <plate> (1.1001)
